Welcome to Lost and Found Web App of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology

How to use:

  1. Login to continue and access the urls.
  2. Search for the lost item on the homepage.
  3. Report the found items here.
    • Click the picture of the found item.
    • Enter the description and location of the item.
    • Submit the found item to the Lost and Found Department.
    • The admin will approve the information about the found item, and the item will be displayed on the website.
  4. If the lost item is not in the list, please send a report here. The admin will take note of it.
  5. Please keep your profile up to date and help us to serve you.

Thank you
